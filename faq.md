## FAQ

### Is VERSA public?

VERSA is a private bot project with public documentation.

### Is self-hosting supported?

No. The project notice indicates self-hosting is not supported.

### Where do I manage server-level bot controls?

Use the official control panel: [Open Control Panel](https://botversa.xyz/)

### Where can I get support?

Use the official [Support Server](https://discord.gg/BusuZp2G8w).
